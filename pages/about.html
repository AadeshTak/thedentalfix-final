Behind The Fix

Every Smile Has a Story. This Is Ours.

The Dental Fix began as more than just a clinic. It started with two passionate dentists, one chair, and a shared belief that great dental care is built on trust, compassion, and genuine connection.

Dr. Ibrahim began his journey in Mumbai, traveling across the city as a specialist in gum surgeries and dental implants. His expertise soon extended to Gujarat, where he consulted at various clinics. A year later, he joined D.Y. Patil Dental School in Pune as a lecturer. While teaching during the day, he continued consulting at leading clinics in the evening, driven by his dedication to hands-on care.

At the same time, Dr. Amreen was building her foundation at 32 Smiles Dental Clinic, under the mentorship of Dr. Milind Darda, one of India's top Invisalign providers. Outside her main role, she served as a consulting orthodontist at multiple clinics, constantly sharpening her clinical skills.

In 2018, their personal and professional journeys aligned. On November 1st, they launched The Dental Fix with a single dental chair, a big dream, and a commitment to ethical and high-quality care. Long hours, late nights, and strong word-of-mouth helped them grow steadily, fueled by ongoing learning and genuine dedication.

The clinic expanded with new equipment, a second chair, and an increasing number of loyal patients who appreciated their warm, honest approach.

In June 2021, they welcomed their daughter Aliza. Dr. Amreen practiced until just three days before delivery and returned to work within a month. During this time, Dr. Ibrahim balanced patient care and fatherhood, often caring for Aliza between appointments.

On June 11, 2023, they opened The Dental Fix 2.0, a larger, state-of-the-art clinic with multiple dental chairs, advanced technology, and a relaxing, patient-first environment. Their team also grew, now including six skilled and compassionate doctors who carry forward the clinic's founding values.

Today, Dr. Amreen continues to serve as a consulting orthodontist while managing the clinic and embracing motherhood. Dr. Ibrahim remains hands-on, caring for patients and mentoring the team. Together, they are committed to the same mission they started with ‚Äî to offer ethical, expert, and empathetic dental care that truly makes patients feel valued.
